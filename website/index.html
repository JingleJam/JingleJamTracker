Over The Years

The Jingle Jam began back in 2011 when Lewis and Simon of The Yogscast asked fans to donate to Oxfam instead of sending them presents. It has since grown to a yearly event where a community of volunteers, content creators, games companies, and gamers from around the world raise money for various causes, including those focused on sustainability, conservation, war relief, accessibility in gaming and sport, cancer research, LGBTQ+ issues, combating racism, and much more.

Our mission has lead to 37 causes from across the world being supported by the Jingle Jam.
